✓ Do

  • Always follow these Brand Guidelines when using EVERPEAK marks and assets.
  • Use the EVERPEAK mark to indicate you are a customer or partner of JServices.
  • Use our marks in a clear, legible, unmodified manner.
  • Use EVERPEAK marks only in digital contexts unless otherwise permitted.
  • Refer to our platform as "EVERPEAK" with proper capitalization.

✕ Don't
