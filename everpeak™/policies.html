Lost or Inaccessible Purchase? Here's How to Recover It

We understand that technology isn't always perfect — files get lost, devices break, storage fails, and downloads sometimes don't go as planned. If you have purchased a photograph from Focal Point Photography and have since lost access to that file, we want to make sure you are able to get what you paid for. Please follow the steps below carefully depending on your situation.

Step 1

Visit the Customer Portal

The first and easiest step is to visit our Customer Portal. This portal is where all of your purchases are stored and available for re-download. To access the portal, you will need to use the exact same email address that you used when you originally completed your checkout. The portal uses a secure, passwordless login system — when you enter your email, a verification link will be sent to that address. You must click that link in your email inbox in order to log in. Once logged in, you will be able to see all purchases associated with that email address and download your files directly from the portal.

Important Note About Email Access: Because the portal login system works by sending a verification link to your email, it is essential that you still have access to the email address you used at checkout. If you no longer have access to that email account — for example, if the account was deleted, if it was a work email you no longer use, or if you simply lost access — the portal verification link will be sent to that inbox and you will not be able to receive it, which means you will not be able to log in through the standard portal process. In this case, please follow the manual verification process described below.
Step 2

Manual Verification (If You Cannot Access Your Portal Email)

If you are unable to access the email address associated with your purchase and therefore cannot log into the customer portal, please send an email to us with the subject line: "Purchase Recovery Request." In your email, you must include the following:

  • Your full name as it was entered at the time of purchase
  • Proof of purchase (the original order confirmation email or receipt you received after completing your checkout — it should include your order details, the date of purchase, and the items purchased)
  • A brief explanation of why you are unable to access the original checkout email

Once we receive your request, we will manually look up your purchase in our records using the information provided. We will verify that the purchase exists and that the details match what is on file. This process may take a short amount of time depending on request volume, so we appreciate your patience. Once your purchase has been successfully verified, we will send your purchased photo directly to you via email — this will be sent to whatever email address you contact us from, so please make sure you are emailing from an address you have full access to and check regularly.

Please note that we take purchase verification seriously and reserve the right to request additional information if we are unable to confirm a purchase with the details initially provided. This process exists to protect both our customers and the integrity of our sales records. We will never knowingly send a purchased file to someone who did not originally purchase it, and we ask for your patience and cooperation in providing the necessary proof so that we can serve you as quickly and accurately as possible. We truly appreciate your support of Focal Point Photography and want to make sure every customer has a positive experience — including being able to access what they paid for, even when things don't go perfectly the first time.
